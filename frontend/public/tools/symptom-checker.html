Symptom Checker

Check your symptoms and get guidance on when to seek medical attention. Remember, this tool is for information only and not a substitute for professional medical advice.

When to Seek Immediate Care

  • Severe abdominal pain
  • High fever (over 39°C/102°F)
  • Severe pain during urination
  • Unusual bleeding

Important Reminders

  • This tool is not a diagnosis
  • Regular testing is recommended
  • Consult healthcare providers
  • Early treatment is important
