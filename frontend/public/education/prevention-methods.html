Prevention Methods

Learn about effective ways to protect yourself and your partners from STIs. Prevention is always better than cure.

Barrier Methods

Options:

  • External condoms
  • Internal condoms
  • Dental dams

Effectiveness:

Up to 98% effective when used correctly

Tips:

  • Check expiration dates
  • Store properly
  • Use for all sexual activities

Regular Testing

When to Test:

  • Every 3-6 months if sexually active
  • With new partners
  • After unprotected sex
  • If experiencing symptoms

Types of Tests:

  • Blood tests
  • Urine tests
  • Physical exams
  • Swab tests

Communication

Discussion Points:

  • Sexual health history
  • Recent STI testing
  • Protection preferences
  • Boundaries and consent

Tips:

  • Be honest and direct
  • Choose private settings
  • Respect boundaries

Additional Prevention Strategies

Vaccinations

  • HPV vaccine recommended for all young people
  • Hepatitis B vaccine important for sexual health
  • Consult healthcare provider about eligibility

Preventive Medications

  • PrEP for HIV prevention
  • PEP for emergency situations
  • Discuss options with healthcare provider
